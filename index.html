LoL MindSet - BETA

Team Fight Tactics Helper & Analyser, Is a Mobile App that helps beginners with picking up Team fight tactics, helping them get better and track their progress. Giving beginners a starting point of what type of champions are strong, what origins and classes are strong, what different items do. Also showing you your current Ranked Stats for Team Fight Tatics. Currentlly in Beta

Features:
-Insight in to winning and losing streaks
-Showing if a player is playing Normally, Well or Great
-Supports Ranked Games on Summoners Rift(Other Games Modes coming soon)



Download Android App
How to Use: When in game use any of the first 3 tabs to help you pick the best team to win , on the 4th tab you can get access to your current ranked stats by entering in your summoner name and region.
